# Quick Start

Get up and running with AchSwap and AchMarket in minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

1. **A Web3 wallet** - MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any RainbowKit-supported wallet
2. **USDC on ARC Testnet** - Get test tokens via the built-in bridge

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

### Using AchSwap
1. Visit [achswap.com](https://achswap.com)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right
3. Select your preferred wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet)
4. Approve the connection request

### Using AchMarket
1. Visit [achmarket.com](https://achmarket.com) (or your deployment URL)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection

## Step 2: Switch to ARC Testnet

When you connect your wallet, you'll be prompted to switch to ARC Testnet if you're not already connected.

**Network Details:**
- Network Name: `ARC Testnet`
- Chain ID: `5042002` (hex: `0x4CEC52`)
- RPC: `https://arc-testnet.drpc.org/`
- Explorer: `https://testnet.arcscan.app`

## Step 3: Get Test USDC

Since ARC Testnet uses USDC as the native gas token, you'll need some for trading:

### Using the Bridge
1. Navigate to **Bridge** on AchSwap
2. Select **Source Chain** (e.g., Ethereum Sepolia)
3. Select **Destination Chain** (ARC Testnet)
4. Enter the USDC amount
5. Click **Bridge** and follow the prompts

### Supported Testnet Bridges
- Ethereum Sepolia
- Avalanche Fuji
- OP Sepolia
- Arbitrum Sepolia
- Base Sepolia
- Polygon Amoy
- Unichain Sepolia
- Linea Sepolia

## Step 4: Start Trading

### On AchSwap
1. Go to the **Swap** page
2. Select your "From" token (e.g., USDC)
3. Select your "To" token (e.g., ACHS)
4. Enter the amount
5. Review the exchange rate and price impact
6. Click **Swap**

### On AchMarket
1. Browse markets on the **Home** page
2. Click on a market to view details
3. Select an outcome to trade
4. Enter your investment amount
5. Review potential payout
6. Click **Buy** to place your trade
